# AI Reliability & Trustworthy AI

This module explores reliability, uncertainty, and explainability in AI systems.

## Topics Covered
- Uncertainty estimation (MC Dropout)
- Calibration (ECE, reliability curves)
- Explainability (Grad-CAM, SHAP)
- Interpretability
- Causality basics

## Motivation
Most AI systems are overconfident and unreliable in real-world settings.  
This module focuses on making models **trustworthy and safe**.

## Goals
- Improve model reliability
- Quantify uncertainty
- Build explainable AI systems

## Techniques
- Temperature scaling
- Gaussian NLL
- Confidence estimation

## Use Cases
- Safety-critical AI (healthcare, wildfire prediction)
